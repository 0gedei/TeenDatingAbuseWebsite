Strategies for Prevention

  • Education: Schools and communities should educate teens about healthy relationships and the signs of abuse.
  • Communication: Encouraging open and honest communication between teens and trusted adults can help identify and address abuse early.
  • Peer Support: Creating peer-led support groups can provide teens with a safe space to discuss their experiences and support each other.

Advocacy

  • Awareness Campaigns: Running campaigns to raise awareness about teen dating abuse can help change attitudes and behaviors.
  • Policy Change: Advocating for policies that protect teens and provide resources for abuse prevention is crucial.
  • Support Services: Ensuring that teens have access to counseling and support services can aid in their recovery and prevention of future abuse.
